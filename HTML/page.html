About us

Chronicle Press helps newsrooms and publishers shape stories with clarity, rigor, and momentum — from first draft to final publication.

We work with editorial teams who refuse to settle for less than their best. Our role is not to replace your voice — it is to sharpen it.

What we believe

Good journalism is not louder journalism. It is clearer journalism. Every project we take on is guided by three commitments: verify before you publish, structure before you polish, and respect the reader’s time.

“We don’t just write. We understand what makes stories stick.”

How we work with clients

Most engagements begin with a conversation about audience, angle, and outcome. From there, we map the editorial work: research support, narrative architecture, line editing, or full project partnership.

  • Editorial strategy and story planning for long-form work
  • Investigative research and fact-checking support
  • Content editing and narrative refinement
  • Publication-ready structure for complex topics

Who we serve

Our clients include regional newsrooms, digital publishers, nonprofit reporting teams, and independent journalists working on investigations, explainers, and narrative features.

Origin

From newsroom floors to editorial partnerships

Chronicle Press was founded by editors and reporters who spent years inside busy newsrooms — and saw how often strong reporting lost impact in the final miles of production.
